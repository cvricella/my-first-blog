About Me and This Site

Hi! My name is Carl Vricella and I am an aspiring environmental scientist who specializes in spatial analysis. I hold a B.S in Environmental Science and I am currently pursuing a Masters Certification in Geo Spatial Science. I have worked for Non-profits doing spatial modeling/analysis and for consulting firms performing figure creation and analysis. This website will be used to blog about GIS, Technology and anything else I might find interesting. Additionally, I will also post tutorials and keep an updated portfolio here. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like me to create a specific tutorial.

Not to worry though, after this project is done and I have some free time I will upload a tutorial on creating a LESA model using ArcMap AND another doing it in QGIS (after I figure that out of course).
